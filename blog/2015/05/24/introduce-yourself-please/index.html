Introduce Yourself Please...

So, this is happened. I was fired.

Hello everyone, my name is Ed Tretyakov, I’m PHP/JS LAMP fullstack dev and this shiny github blog is a little story about my life after breaking with my Dreamjob. I was working for DeviantArt little bit more that 1 year and was fired less than a month ago. And this was not about my skills or exp as my manager said - company just refocusing it needs. And I don’t needed anymore. Sad smile must be here, lol.

Why i’m here?

From the beginning this blog main purpose is to keep my head cool and one more try to analyze a little my mistakes in the long journey to search a new job (I hope it will be my next Dreamjob). To be honest with every failed attempt my mood get lower and lower and I just start to think that somethnig is wrong with me.

One more important thing (but I did not expect too much here) - maybe someone from companies, who answered to me with denial of single damn attempt with some easy-to-send template emails (from last emails - like Wikimedia talent search team, who answered with “we have decided not to proceed with your candidacy” and Formstack, who answered with “we have chosen to pursue other candidates that better fit the needs of the role.”) will see one day my posts and feel my pain too. Because it’s fuckin hard to read this stuff without simple piece of understanding, where exactly I’m failed. CV not interesting? Low experience? Not perfect language? Wrong skin color? Magnetic storms? Anything else? Nobody answer. And all this templates are sended from no-reply@… So, while talking to companies in the usual way is hard - maybe this blog helps? At least I hope so…

And as for me this is really big problem. Ain’t nobody give a single fuckin chance to proove yor skills or experience or passion to coding and engineering. Nobody. So far I seen only one company (CurrencyFair) who ask for some kind of test and while in the test submit form they promised to answer in 2 days - week passed and still nobody visit it.

I will try to analyze what I’ve done so far and what steps was processed with every company I’m tried. It’s interesting and if I can design and create some things in the process - that will be really awesome. And, yeah, I don’t want to freeze and want to express somethnig new to me too, so why not? Freezed, bored, tired and upset programmer is dead for community and market, and I don’t want to be dat dead thing.

I want to close this blog when my job will find me, but who knows? Maybe I will like it and just change a title.

Stay tuned.

P.S. Sorry for my english, I live in Ukraine and not native speaker, so sometimes my language can be really bad.
